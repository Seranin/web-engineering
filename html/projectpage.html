Our Current Project: Jack's Adventure

Are you unsatisfied with the current landscape of pirate games? Do you think that the pirate game genre peaked with AC : Black flag in 2014? Do you want to feel the same excitement again? Well, fear not! We are proud to announce Jack's Adventure, coming Q2 of 2023. Developed by Akita productions, a 2 man dev team from Thomas-more ICT department (Phase 1, App Dev), Jack's Adventure is the pirate game you have been waiting for! Trade, loot & plunder till your hearts content! Developed in Unity, this top down RPG is going to rock your world!
